About Cecconi's

Our Story, Our Passion, Our Heritage

Our Story

Our journey began in 1978 when the first Cecconi's opened its doors in London's prestigious Mayfair district. Born from a vision to bring authentic Venetian dining beyond Italy's borders, we've grown from a single location to a celebrated collection of restaurants spanning the globe.

What started as an intimate dining experience featuring classic Northern Italian dishes has evolved into an international destination for those seeking genuine Italian hospitality. Each Cecconi's location honors our heritage while embracing the unique character of its neighborhood.

Here in Brooklyn Heights, we continue this tradition at 55 Water Street, bringing decades of culinary expertise to New York. Our menu celebrates time-honored recipes alongside innovative interpretations, creating memorable experiences for every guest who joins our table.

Our Mission

Authentic Italian

We stay true to traditional Italian cooking methods and use only the finest imported ingredients to create authentic flavors that transport you to Italy.

Family Tradition

Food brings families together. We create a warm, welcoming environment where every guest feels like part of our extended Italian family.

Fresh Ingredients

We source the freshest local produce and premium Italian imports daily, ensuring every dish meets our high standards of quality and taste.

Prime Brooklyn Heights Location

Nestled in the heart of Brooklyn Heights at 55 Water Street, Cecconi's offers more than exceptional dining—it provides breathtaking views and access to some of New York's most iconic attractions.

Step outside our doors and discover the stunning Brooklyn Bridge Park, where the Manhattan skyline creates a dramatic backdrop for your evening. The waterfront promenade offers unparalleled views of the East River and Brooklyn Bridge.

Our neighborhood boasts world-class attractions including the exclusive SOHO House, the charming Jane's Carousel, and the historic Brooklyn Heights Promenade—all within walking distance of your table.

Discover Brooklyn Heights

Brooklyn Bridge Park

85-acre waterfront park featuring stunning Manhattan skyline views, gardens, and recreational facilities just steps from our restaurant.

SOHO House Brooklyn

Exclusive members' club housed in a historic warehouse, offering rooftop views and sophisticated atmosphere in our vibrant neighborhood.

Jane's Carousel

Historic 1922 carousel beautifully restored and housed in a stunning glass pavilion, creating magical moments for visitors of all ages.
